Features of Flourish Blockchain

The proud features that make flourish blockchain unique among its peers and guarantees mass adoption in the near future.

Fully Crypto Backed

Basket of Fiat currencies backed by FLB coin and established stablecoins such as Statis EUR and USDT.

Delegated Proof of Stake

Flourish blockchain leverages the delegated prood of stake of the phantom blockchain allowing holders of FLB to delegate holdings and earn rewards.

Over-collaterized

Synthetic stablecoins are over collaterized making the possiblity of liquidation extremely possible.
