What is Flourish Blockchain

Flourish Blockchain is a highly scalable blockchain designed for the purpose of personal finance, its major aim is to provide an access to cryptocurrency for individuals to take responsibility of their finances. Free from every form of centralized control and authority. This method provides the opportunity that was once reserved for high net worth individuals and institutions to create wealth to anyone who is ready to take hold of their personal finance.

We already have many blockchains how is Flourish Blockchain different and why should I get involved

It is true we already have many blockchain projects that are decentralized and opensource but most often these projects are focused on the needs of people in western region while other people from other continents and regions are neglected, excluded or given a very hard condition to enjoy the same level of participation. This is why Flourish Blockchain is developed to give access to every neglected communities, region, countries and continents on the face of earth. All our technology and solutions will be designed with this motive in mind making it easy and accessible to everyone.

How will the Flourish stablecoins work

Flourish Blockchain will have a basket of synthetic assets which are stable coins of different underserved countries like Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Botswana, Tanzania, Pakistan, Philippine, South Africa and many more. These stable coins will be backed by flourish coins with at least 100% collateral ratio making the possibility of liquidation extremely difficult and attack from the government virtually impossible. Anyone and Everyone will have the opportunity to mint, trade, borrow and lend these synthetic assets.

What is the purpose of FLB coin

Flourish coin uses a Proof-of-Stake system that requires validators to hold some FLB. By locking their FLB, validators help the network to be decentralized and protected against 51% of attacks. It will also act as a gorvenance token which enables holders to participate in gorvenance and earn rewards.

Paying Network Fees

To compensate validators for their service and prevent transaction spam, every action performed within the Flourish network costs a small fee which will be paid in FLB

Voting and On-Chain Governance

Decisions regarging the Flourish ecosysytem are made using transparent on-chain voting. Votes are weighted according to the amount of FLB held by an entity. Basically 1 FLB equals 1 vote. With FLB as the gorvenence token validators and delegators can vote on which stable coin to add to our basket of synthetic assets, block rewwards as well as technical updates of the protocol and so forth.

Future Use Cases

FLB will be used as collateral on the upcoming Flourish DeFi suite that would be developed in the future rodmap.
